Welcome to Web Design Principles

Learn to create visually appealing and accessible web pages.

Why Web Design Matters

Good design improves user experience, communicates your message clearly, and supports your business objectives.

Core Design Principles

Typography

Use 2–3 fonts, focus on readability, and apply font weights for variation.

Color

Limit your palette to 5 colors and ensure sufficient contrast for accessibility.

Clarity & Consistency

Keep your layout clean, use whitespace wisely, and maintain a consistent visual style.

Quick Tips

  • Use grid or flexbox for layout.
  • Replace outdated elements like marquee with static banners.
  • Check accessibility with tools like WAVE or Lighthouse.
  • Test on multiple devices and screen sizes.
