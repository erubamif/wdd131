Paris, France

About Paris

Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture.

Its 19th-century cityscape features wide boulevards and the River Seine. Famous landmarks include the Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame Cathedral.

The city is also known for its café culture and designer boutiques.

  • Louvre Museum
