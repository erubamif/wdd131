Local Food & Recipes

Naija Flavors

This name represents a collection of Nigerian traditional recipes and highlights the rich flavors of Nigerian cuisine. It is simple, memorable, and culturally relevant.

Optional Domain: naijaflavors.com

Site Purpose

The website provides information about Nigerian traditional dishes, including recipes, ingredients, and cooking instructions. It also allows users to explore different types of meals and learn how to prepare them at home.

Scenarios

  • How do I cook Jollof Rice step by step?
  • What ingredients are needed for Egusi Soup?
  • Which Nigerian dishes are easy for beginners?

Color Schema

  • Red (#c1121f): Used for headings and highlights.
  • Green (#2a9d8f): Used for accents and buttons.
  • Light Background (#f8f9fa): Used for page background.

Typography

  • Arial: Used for body text.
  • Georgia: Used for headings.
